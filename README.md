# 🎬 Netflix Content Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

## 📖 Project Overview

This project analyzes Netflix's content library to understand how its catalog has evolved over time and identify factors influencing audience engagement across Movies and TV Shows. Using an interactive Power BI dashboard, the analysis explores content growth trends, audience ratings, runtime patterns, and content certifications to uncover insights that can support data-driven content strategy decisions.

---

## 📂 Dataset

The analysis is based on **5,283 Netflix titles** and includes the following attributes:

* Title Name
* Content Type (Movie / TV Show)
* IMDb Rating
* Audience Votes
* Runtime
* Release Year
* Certification Rating
* Genre Information

## 📊 Key Analysis

### Content Growth Analysis

* Examined how Netflix's content library expanded over time.
* Identified peak years of content production.

### Movies vs TV Shows Comparison

* Compared audience engagement across Movies and TV Shows.
* Evaluated differences in IMDb ratings and popularity.

### Audience Engagement Analysis

* Analyzed IMDb ratings and audience votes to identify highly popular titles.
* Investigated factors driving viewer preferences.

### Runtime Trend Analysis

* Studied changes in average content runtime over multiple decades.
* Evaluated shifts in audience consumption patterns.

### Content Certification Analysis

* Assessed the impact of content certifications on audience engagement.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Netflix content production peaked at **749 titles in 2019**, indicating rapid expansion of the content library.
* TV Shows achieved **12% higher IMDb ratings** than Movies, suggesting stronger audience engagement.
* **Forrest Gump** emerged as the highest-rated classic movie with an IMDb rating of **8.8** and nearly **1.99 million audience votes**.
* Average content runtime declined by **43% over six decades**, reflecting changing viewer preferences.
* Audience engagement increasingly favors shorter and highly rated content.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Increase investment in high-performing TV Show content to maximize audience engagement.
* Focus on producing shorter, binge-friendly content aligned with changing viewing habits.
* Leverage historical audience ratings and votes to guide future content acquisition decisions.
* Prioritize genres and certifications associated with higher engagement levels.
* Use content performance metrics to optimize production budgets and release strategies.

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard that provides a comprehensive view of Netflix's content evolution and audience engagement patterns. The analysis uncovered trends in content production, viewer preferences, and title performance, enabling data-driven decisions for content strategy and audience retention.
